Domain Names

A domain name is the human-readable address of a website.

For example: example.com, google.com, rvu.edu.in

Parts of a Domain

  • www = subdomain
  • example = domain name
  • .com = top-level domain

Common TLDs

  • .com = commercial
  • .org = organization
  • .edu = education
  • .in = India
