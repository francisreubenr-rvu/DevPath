🃏 Printable Game Components

📋 Printing Instructions:

  • Paper: Use 300gsm cardstock for cards, 200gsm for tokens and prompts
  • Size: Print on A4 paper, one section per page
  • Cutting: Cut carefully along borders. Cards are poker-sized (63×88mm)
  • Optional: Use card sleeves for role cards to hide card backs
  • Lamination: Recommended for tokens and prompts (frequent handling)
