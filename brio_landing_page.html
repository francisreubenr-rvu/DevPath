Features

Designed for builders,
not browsers.

Every feature exists to make you ship faster and learn deeper — nothing else.

01

AI learning paths

Project-scoped curriculum tied directly to your Kanban tasks. Lessons map to exactly what your product needs next — nothing generic.

02

Skill-gated Kanban

Task cards are locked at the database layer until you pass the prerequisite lesson. Score 70 or above on the AI evaluation to unlock.

03

AI code evaluation

Submit your code and receive a structured score with actionable feedback. A passing score fires a database trigger that unlocks your next task.

04

Team workspaces

Form teams of 2–4 with role assignments — lead, backend, frontend, devops. One shared Kanban board. One product. Shipped together.

05

Ship It milestones

All tasks done? Project status flips to Shipped via a DB trigger. Your portfolio gains a real case study — not a certificate with no proof attached.

06

Blueprint engine

Before you write your first line, AI prescribes a curated tech stack and scoped project spec. Clarity before the first commit.

The Brio Philosophy
The core mechanic

The skill gate.

Your Kanban board is not a to-do list. Every card is a locked capability — unlocked only when you prove mastery of the prerequisite lesson with a passing AI score.

Enforced via a MySQL AFTER INSERT trigger. The rule lives in the database — no application layer can bypass it.

Process

From idea to shipped
in four steps.

01

Blueprint

Tell Brio what you want to build. AI prescribes a tech stack, scopes the project, and creates your Kanban board before you write a single line.

02

Learn

Work through your AI-generated path. Each lesson maps to a locked task. Submit code, earn a passing score, watch the gate open.

03

Build

Move through your Kanban board as capabilities unlock. Solo or with a team — one board, one product, total accountability.

04

Ship

All tasks complete — project flips to Shipped via a DB trigger. A portfolio case study is generated. Not a badge. A product.
